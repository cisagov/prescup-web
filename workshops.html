Expand Your Cyber Skills
with Hands-On Training

The President’s Cup Cybersecurity Workshops provide an interactive and immersive experience designed to help participants refine their cybersecurity expertise. Whether you’re looking to enhance existing skills, gain practical experience, or collaborate with peers, these workshops offer a unique opportunity to engage with customized challenges tailored to your objectives.

To request a workshop, please review the details below and reach out to the President’s Cup team.

Workshop Details

Why Participate in a Workshop?

  • Interactive Learning: Engage with customized challenges designed to meet your team’s specific goals and objectives. These challenges are adapted from President’s Cup components and tailored to align with your unique needs.
  • Skill Development: Build, refine, and test your cybersecurity knowledge in a hands-on environment.
  • Collaborative Environment: Work alongside peers to solve challenges, exchange ideas, and learn from each other.
  • Expert Insights: Gain valuable tips, strategies, and real-world applications from cybersecurity professionals.
  • Earn Credits: Receive a Certificate of Participation that can be used to claim Continuing Professional Education (CPE) or Continuing Professional Units (CPU) credits.

Workshop Formats

Customized Challenge Debrief & Walkthrough
  • Participants attempt tailored challenges independently over a set period.
  • Facilitators host a debrief session to review solutions, share insights, and answer questions.
Guided Customized Challenge Walkthrough
  • Facilitators walk participants through a specific tailored challenge from start to finish, providing step-by-step guidance and explanations.
Mini-Competitions (Coming Soon - Summer 2026)
  • Host a mini-competition where participants compete individually or in teams to solve curated and customized challenges within a set timeframe.
  • Facilitators oversee the competition, provide real-time support, and conclude with a debrief to review solutions and announce top performers.
  • Includes leaderboards, certificates, and optional recognition for winners.

What Makes Our Workshops Unique?

  • Tailored Content: Challenges are customized to align with your team’s skill levels, objectives, and areas of focus, ensuring a relevant and impactful experience.
  • Real-World Scenarios: Engage with challenges designed to simulate real-world cybersecurity incidents and problem-solving.
  • Networking Opportunities: Build connections with peers and cybersecurity professionals during collaborative sessions.
  • Customizable Experience: Select challenges and formats that align with your team’s goals for maximum value.

Challenges

With over 290 challenges to choose from the Practice Area*, both Individuals (with defensive and offensive tracks) and Teams challenges are available with various levels of difficulty.

All challenges are aligned to work roles from the NICE Framework. These include:

  • Cyberspace Operations
  • Data Analysis
  • Defensive Cybersecurity
  • Digital Forensics
  • Exploitation Analysis
  • Incident Response
  • Infrastructure Support
  • Network Operations
  • Secure Software Development
  • Technology Research and Development
  • Threat Analysis
  • Vulnerability Analysis

You may request specific challenges from the Practice Area or targeted NICE Work Roles/skillsets based upon your preference.

  • Within the site, challenges labeled "Track A" denote defensive cybersecurity skills, and "Track B" denote offensive cybersecurity skills.
  • You can also filter challenges based upon their skillsets by clicking on the corresponding labels under the "Need a place to start?" heading.

*Please note that descriptions and solution guides for all previous President's Cup challenges are publicly available via the Github Repository. This may be used as a resource to help enhance the learning of the workshop participants

How to Request a Workshop

Getting started is easy!

  • Complete Workshop Request Form (Coming Soon): The form allows you to specify your objectives, preferred format, and other details to help us tailor the workshop to your needs.
  • Email Us Directly: Contact us at presidentscup@cisa.dhs.gov with your request.
    Please include your name, organization, preferred format, and any specific goals or topics you’d like to address.

Our team will reach out directly to confirm details and schedule workshop.

Please Note: In-person workshops are available on a limited basis and are subject to facilitator availability and location constraints. Virtual workshops are highly encouraged and offer the same level of customization and interactivity.

Once we receive your request, a member of our team will reach out to finalize the details and ensure the workshop is customized to meet your needs.

Who Can Participate?

Workshops are open to federal employees, students, and professionals seeking to enhance their cybersecurity skills.

Established to identify, recognize, and reward the best cyber talent across the federal workforce, the President's Cup Cybersecurity Competition challenges participants to outthink and outwit their competitors in a series of cyber challenges based on real-world situations.

Ranging from defending networks to analyzing threats and uncovering vulnerabilities, the competition highlights the importance of cybersecurity readiness, encourages professional growth, and showcases the skills of those working to keep our nation secure. Only the sharpest minds will make it through two virtual qualifying rounds to the finals in the National Capital Region.

Now in its seventh year, the President's Cup continues to foster collaboration, raise awareness of cybersecurity careers, and recognize the dedication of the professionals who protect our digital infrastructure.

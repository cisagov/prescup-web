Established to identify, recognize, and reward the best cyber talent across the federal workforce, the President’s Cup Cybersecurity Competition challenges participants to outthink and outwit their competitors in a series of cyber challenges based on real-world situations. Packed with covert operations and encrypted intel, President’s Cup 7 offers you the opportunity to identify threats, sharpen skills, and rise through the ranks.

Only the sharpest minds will make it through two virtual qualifying rounds to the finals in the National Capital Region. Are you ready to accept your mission? Registration opens December 8, 2025.
